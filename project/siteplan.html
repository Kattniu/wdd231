Milan Explorer - Site Plan

1. SITE NAME

Proposed Name: Milan Explorer

Reason for Selection: The name reflects the purpose of providing an engaging and comprehensive guide to Milan, offering information on the best places to eat, visit, and experience in the city.

Optional Domain Availability: milanexplorer.com

2. SITE PURPOSE

The purpose of the site is to provide a complete and interactive guide for tourists visiting Milan, focusing on:

  • Restaurants in Milan: Where to eat in the city, from fine dining to street food.
  • Museums and Art: Discovering Milan's rich cultural heritage.
  • Must-See Sights: Iconic landmarks and hidden gems in Milan.
  • Tips for Travelers: Helpful advice to ensure a smooth and enjoyable trip.

3. SCENARIOS

Scenario 1: A tourist planning a 3-day trip to Milan, searching for the best places to eat and visit.

Scenario 2: A first-time visitor to Italy wanting to explore Milan's culture and art scene.

Scenario 3: A business traveler in Milan with limited time, looking for quick sightseeing tips and efficient routes.

Scenario 4: A traveler seeking advice on local customs to ensure they blend in and have an authentic experience in Milan.

4. COLOR SCHEMA

Primary Color: #B03A2E (Red Earth) - Used for headings, buttons, and prominent elements to draw attention.

Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) - For body text to provide high contrast and legibility.

Secondary Color: #6A7F3D (Olive Green) - Used for secondary elements like links, icons, and hover effects.

Accent Color: #BDC3C7 (Light Gray) - Used for special highlights, borders, and subtle details.

Background Color: #FFFFFF (White) - Used for most backgrounds to ensure readability and a clean, modern look.

5. TYPOGRAPHY

Font 1: Montserrat - Used for headings and titles, offering a bold, modern feel in Red color (#D91C2E).

Font 2: Open Sans - Used for body text, offering excellent readability in Black (#333333) or Grey (#D1D0C6).

Font 3: Lora (Italic) - Used for quotes, advice, and tips, providing a classic, elegant touch in Green (#008C45) or Grey (#D1D0C6).
